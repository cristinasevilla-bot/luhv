HOW TO GET THE MOST
From the book to your daily life — here is how Luhv+ works.
📍
Today — Your daily dashboard
Start here every morning. Check your Effectiveness Score, see your Peak Window, review today's habits and get your coach message.
🎯
Goals — Your 90-day roadmap
Set 2-3 big goals. Use + and − to update progress. The coach tracks your momentum and calls you out if you stall.
✅
Habits — Daily non-negotiables
Add habits you do every day. Use Daily check for yes/no habits, or Daily target for things like glasses of water. Streak builds here.
⏱️
Focus — Protect your time
Use Deep Work (25min) during your Peak Window for maximum output. The timer notifies you when done, even if you leave the app.
⚡
Coach — Your AI accountability partner
Ask anything. The coach knows your goals, habits and history. Use it when stuck, planning your week, or needing a push. Messages can be copied and shared.
🎯
Decision Check — Hidden gem
Before making any decision, tap the purple button on the left. The coach will tell you if it aligns with your goals — instantly.
📈
Progress — See your growth
Weekly review from the coach, energy tracking, peak performance report and full check-in log. Check this on Sundays.
⚡
Peak Performance Window
Set your preferred working hours in Profile (top right). The Creativity window is 5am-12pm — the best time for deep work. Aspire to shift your schedule toward it.
